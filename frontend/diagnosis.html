Maintenance & Troubleshooting Guide

Dust Accumulation

Optical gain loss due to surface fouling.

  • Clean collector glass with soft cloth.
  • Automate cleaning in high-dust regions.

Heat Leakage

Thermal energy escaping through insulation.

  • Re-wrap exposed pipe segments.
  • Check valve seals and joint integrity.

Pump Degradation

Reduced flow rate preventing heat transfer.

  • Inspect pump impeller for scaling.
  • Increase pump duty cycle or replace motor.

Sensor Drift

Inaccurate temperature readings (Scale Drift).

  • Check for wire corrosion at junctions.
  • Initiate electronic re-calibration script.

Low Efficiency

Generalized performance drop below 40%.

  • Comprehensive system inspection required.
  • Check for scaling in all subsystems.
